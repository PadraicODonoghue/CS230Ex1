NUIM Broadcasting Society


I am the IT and Technology guy for NUIMs Broadcasting Society. MarsFM.ie is a product of the society. Our aim is for MarsFM to become a radio station for students by students. We aim to promote local talent and provide experience to students of the college in media, broadcasting and station management. I am in charge of maintaining the stations website and studio aswell as training for new members of the society who want to get involved.
